CheezCut — concept overview

CheezCut is an early-stage product concept designed to make it easier to remove the outer packaging or seal around pizza cheese. The idea is simple: reduce mess, reduce unsafe handling, and remove the need to use hands or a knife for a repetitive kitchen task.

Status:
- Invented by Amir Mobasheraghdam
- Concept / early presentation stage
- Not currently for sale

Potential value:
- Safer workflow
- Faster opening process
- Cleaner handling in busy kitchens
- Better consistency for restaurants and pizza preparation environments
