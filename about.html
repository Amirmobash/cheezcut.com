About the project

CheezCut is positioned as an inventor-led concept by Amir Mobasheraghdam. The project focuses on a narrow but real task: removing the red wax rind around Gouda more effectively than the usual hand-only routine.

Why this idea matters

Good product ideas do not always begin with large machines or complex mechanisms. Sometimes they begin with a small, repeated point of friction in everyday work. CheezCut comes from exactly that logic.

When Gouda is handled in the real world, the outer red layer is often removed by hand. That process can be slow, inconsistent, or simply less refined than it should be. CheezCut reframes that moment as a design opportunity.

“The goal is not to reinvent cheese preparation. The goal is to improve one small, repeated step so it feels faster, cleaner, and more intentional.”

Amir Mobasheraghdam · Concept positioning

Brand position

CheezCut is presented professionally, but honestly. It is not framed as a finished mass-market product. It is framed as a concept-stage utility tool that is ready for presentation, discussion, refinement, and early interest.

  • Inventor-led, not anonymous corporate positioning
  • Focused utility, not a broad kitchen gadget claim
  • Presentation-ready, not checkout-ready
  • Clear about what the concept does and does not do
