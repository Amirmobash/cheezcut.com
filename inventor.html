About the inventor

Amir Mobasheraghdam

CheezCut is presented as an inventor-led concept by Amir Mobasheraghdam. This page introduces the engineering mindset behind the project without listing a current employer or current workplace.

Engineering, product thinking, and practical invention.

Amir Mobasheraghdam is presented publicly as a mechanical engineer with a strong focus on industrial design, automation, and practical concept development. Across his public profile material, the emphasis stays on building useful systems, refining interactions, and turning technical ideas into clearer product directions.

Mechanical engineering Industrial design Automation mindset Prototype-oriented thinking

Design tools

Public profile material points to CAD- and build-oriented workflows, including Siemens NX and hands-on industrial design thinking.

Automation

The broader body of public posts and projects reflects sustained interest in automation, robotic systems, and practical engineering improvement.

Digital experimentation

Public posts also highlight work around computer vision, AI-assisted inspection, and prototype-driven technical exploration.

Inventor approach

CheezCut fits that same pattern: identify one friction point, define the interaction clearly, and present the concept with discipline.

Practical engineering first

The founder narrative behind CheezCut is not based on broad lifestyle branding. It is based on noticing a repeatable task and improving it.

Prototype-led thinking

The project is presented as a concept that can be discussed, refined, prototyped, and evaluated with real-world use in mind.

Focused communication

The concept is intentionally framed as a utility for Gouda wax-rind removal rather than stretched into unrelated claims.

“I wanted the founder page to feel engineering-led: clear, disciplined, and focused on how ideas become useful tools.”
